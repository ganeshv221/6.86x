Clustering
----------
Calculating costs
-----------------
Sum of Eucledian distance 
Cost(C1) = ||x1-z1||^2 + ||x2-z1||^2 + ||x3-z1||^2 
= ||(-1,2)-(-1,1)||^2 + ||(-2,1)-(-1,1)||^2 + ||(-1,0)-(-1,1)||^2
= 1 + 1 + 1 = 3
Cost(C2) = ||x4-z2||^2 + ||x5-z2||^2
= ||(2,1)-(2,2)||^2 + ||(3,2)-(2,2)||^2
= 1 + 1 = 2
Cost(C1,C2) = Cost(C1) + Cost(C2)
= 5
